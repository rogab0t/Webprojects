NEON GENESIS EVANGELION

Neon Genesis Evangelion is an anime series that originally aired in Japan in 1995. The plot follows Shinji Ikari, a young man who is called upon by his father to pilot a giant robot called Evangelion in order to fight against beings called Angels that threaten the survival of humanity.

In summary, Neon Genesis Evangelion is a complex and philosophical anime series that follows a young man who pilots a giant robot to fight against beings called Angels. The original series consists of 26 episodes, followed by the movie "The End of Evangelion". The Rebuild of Evangelion movies offer a reimagining of the original story and present a slightly different plot.

Rei Ayanami

She is a skilled pilot of the EVAs, and her apparent lack of emotions often makes her appear cold and distant. However, as the series progresses, details about her past and her true identity are revealed, making her one of the most interesting and complex characters in Evangelion.

Shinji Ikari

Shinji Ikari is the protagonist of Evangelion, an introverted and emotionally vulnerable young man who is summoned by his father to pilot an EVA. He often struggles with his self-esteem and has difficulty connecting with others, which leads him to feel lonely and isolated.

Despite his apparent weakness, Shinji demonstrates great courage and determination as he fights to survive in an increasingly dangerous and chaotic world.
